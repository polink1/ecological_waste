About Us


Why you should go E-co Logic?

With rising levels of e-waste across the globe, Earth is facing the danger of excessive enviornmental pollution. Furthmore, due to inevitable inncrease in the usage of VR and AI equipment, the amount of e-waste generated is only going to escalate. Using our tools at E-co Logic, you are able to read articles to spread awareness about e-waste management, locate an e-waste recycling facility near you, and use our door-to-door pickup feature to arrange pickups from your location in an efficient way.

Here's what we do!

1. Share articles about e-waste management to keep people aware of the concerning issue.
2. Help locate a recycling facility so that anyone can contribute to making our home a better place .
3. Arrange pick ups from your location so it is easy and accessible to recycle your old devices.
